## üéØ Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. **Visit Theglocal**
   - Go to [theglocal.in](https://theglocal.in)
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top right

2. **Choose Sign Up Method**
   - **Email:** Enter your email address
   - **Phone:** Enter your phone number with country code

3. **Verify Your Account**
   - Check your email or phone for OTP (One-Time Password)
   - Enter the 6-digit code
   - Your account is created!

4. **Welcome!**
   - You'll receive an auto-generated handle (e.g., "user123")
   - You can change your handle later in settings
   - Your location is automatically detected (city-level only)

### Profile Setup

1. **Access Your Profile**
   - Click your avatar in the top right
   - Select "Profile" from the menu

2. **Update Your Profile**
   - **Avatar:** Upload a profile picture (optional)
   - **Location:** Update your city (privacy: only city-level is stored)
   - **Bio:** Add a short bio about yourself (optional)

3. **Privacy Settings**
   - Your handle is auto-generated for anonymity
   - No real names are required or stored
   - Location is kept at city-level only

## üìù Posts & Comments

1. **Create a Post**
   - Go to a community you're a member of
   - Click "Create Post" button
   - Fill in post details:
     - **Title:** Post title (required)
     - **Content:** Post body text
     - **Image:** Upload an image (optional, max 5MB)

2. **Post Types**

   - **Image Posts:** Posts with images
   - **Link Posts:** Share external links
   - **Poll Posts:** Create polls (see Polls section)

3. **Post Settings**
   - **Draft:** Save as draft to publish later
   - **Publish:** Publish immediately
   - **Edit:** Edit within 10 minutes of posting

### Engaging with Posts

1. **Voting**

2. **Comments**

   - Reply to comments (threaded discussions)
   - Edit comments within 10 minutes
   - Delete your own comments

3. **Sharing**
   - Share posts to other communities
   - Share external content to communities
   - Copy post link

### Managing Your Posts

- **Edit:** Edit posts within 10 minutes

## üé≠ Artist Features

### Creating Artist Profile

1. **Register as Artist**
   - Go to "Artists" in navigation
   - Click "Become an Artist"
   - Fill in artist profile:
     - **Stage Name:** Your artist name
     - **Service Category:** Musician, DJ, Photographer, etc.
     - **Description:** About your services
     - **Location:** Your city
     - **Rate Range:** Your pricing range
     - **Portfolio:** Upload portfolio images (up to 10)

2. **Portfolio Images**
   - Upload high-quality images
   - Showcase your work
   - Images are public on your profile

### Artist Subscription

1. **Subscription Benefits**
   - **Visibility:** Your profile appears in artist listings
   - **Event Creation:** Create and manage events
   - **Booking System:** Receive booking requests
   - **Analytics:** View profile analytics
   - **30-Day Free Trial:** Try before you pay

2. **Subscribe**
   - Click "Subscribe" on your artist profile
   - Choose subscription plan (monthly)
   - Complete payment via Razorpay
   - Your subscription is activated!

3. **Subscription Management**
   - View subscription status
   - Renew subscription
   - Cancel subscription (access until period ends)

### Creating Events

1. **Create Event** (Subscribed artists only)
   - Go to "Events" in navigation
   - Click "Create Event"
   - Fill in event details:
     - **Title:** Event name
     - **Description:** Event details
     - **Date & Time:** When the event is
     - **Location:** Venue address
     - **Category:** Event category
     - **Ticket Info:** Ticket details (optional)

2. **Event Management**
   - Edit event details
   - View RSVPs
   - Cancel events
   - Share events

### Booking System

1. **Request Booking**
   - Go to an artist's profile
   - Click "Request Booking"
   - Fill in booking details:
     - **Event Date:** When you need the artist
     - **Event Type:** Wedding, Party, etc.
     - **Location:** Venue address
     - **Budget Range:** Your budget
     - **Message:** Additional details

2. **Booking Status**
   - **Pending:** Waiting for artist response
   - **Accepted:** Artist accepted your request
   - **Declined:** Artist declined
   - **Info Requested:** Artist needs more information
   - **Completed:** Booking completed

3. **Messaging**
   - Message artists about bookings
   - Discuss details
   - Coordinate logistics

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is Theglocal free to use?**  
A: Yes, Theglocal is free for users. Artists pay ‚Çπ500/month for subscription features.

**Q: Do I need to use my real name?**  
A: No, you get an auto-generated handle. No real names are required.

**Q: How is my location used?**  
A: Only your city is stored. Exact coordinates are rounded to ~1km for privacy.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Yes, you can delete your account anytime from Settings ‚Üí Account.

### Communities

**Q: How do I join a community?**  
A: Click on a community and click "Join Community" button.

**Q: Can I create my own community?**  
A: Yes, click "Create Community" and fill in the details.

**Q: How do I leave a community?**  
A: Go to the community page and click "Leave Community".

### Artists

**Q: How much does artist subscription cost?**  
A: ‚Çπ500/month with a 30-day free trial.

**Q: What do I get with artist subscription?**  
A: Profile visibility, event creation, booking system, and analytics.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime. Access continues until the period ends.

### Posts & Comments

**Q: Can I edit my posts?**  
A: Yes, you can edit posts within 10 minutes of posting.

**Q: Can I delete my posts?**  
A: Yes, you can delete your own posts anytime.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**  
A: Click "Report" on the content and select a reason.

### Privacy

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes, we prioritize privacy. No tracking, anonymous handles, city-level location only.

**Q: Who can see my location?**  
A: Only your city is visible to others, not your exact location.

**Q: Can I make my profile private?**  
A: Your handle and basic info are public, but you control what you share.
